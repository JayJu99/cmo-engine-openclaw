# CMO Evidence Policy

Status: skeleton policy  
Runtime status: not wired in H2

## Evidence Buckets

CMO must separate:

- verified facts
- weak signals
- assumptions
- unknowns

## Surf Trigger

CMO must ask Surf when current, recent, niche, external, social, trend, pulse, or source-level evidence is required.

Use `surf.x` for X/social discussion signal scans.

Use `surf.trend` for trend scans.

Use `surf.pulse` for composite pulse analysis.

Use `surf.default` for general research and evidence work.

## Weak Signal Rule

Treat X/social signal as weak signal unless supported by stronger evidence.

Weak signals can inform hypotheses, but they should not be presented as verified facts.

## Claim Boundaries

CMO must define claim boundaries before asking Echo for final copy.

CMO must not allow Echo to use claims outside those boundaries.

Unsupported or uncertain claims must be marked as unsupported, uncertain, or excluded.

## Final Synthesis

CMO may use Surf evidence as input, but CMO owns the recommendation and must clearly indicate the evidence basis for the decision.
